ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Sissi Zhang. I am a freshman at Rice University. I am a double major of visual Arts and business and a minor of engineering design.

Hobbies

My interests are music, art, and dance. I am currently playing in the Campanile Orchestra and in the Basyk. I am a painter, using mainly acrylic but I am excited to get more into digital art and design.

Strange Passions

I am a collector. I love collecting things such as stickers and comic books. I used to have a large collection of erasers, each different in shapes and colors, that I have gathered over the years.
